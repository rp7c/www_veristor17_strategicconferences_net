Hotel

Paradisus Los Cabos
Carretera Transpeninsular Km 19.5

San José del Cabo, Baja California Sur

Situated on a private crescent cove beach Paradisus Los Cabos is a luxury all inclusive resort surrounded by lush gardens and exquisite touches.

VeriStor has arranged special hotel room rates with the Paradisus Los Cabos during the event. If you would like to extend your visit, the negotiated room rate is $202 per person per night for double occupancy ($404 for two people in one room per night)--this is an all-inclusive per person rate per night. We ask the participants not to contact the hotel directly.

The regular check-in time at the hotel is 3:00PM. An earlier check-in is subject to availability. The official check-out time of the hotel is 12:00 pm. Luggage storage will be provided through the bell desk at the hotel.

Other Information

Airline arrangements:Within 48 hours of completing your registration you will be contacted by a VeriStor air reservation specialist who will help book your flights. Please do not book your own flight without written approval from the logistical team.

Airport Transportation To/From: Round trip airport transportation to/from Los Cabos International Airport (SJD) to Paradisus Los Cabos will be provided. You will meet the transportation team once you retrieve your luggage. More information will be provided prior to the trip. Transportation will be provided back to the airport.

Attire: Dress according to activity selections, please see activity list for specific dress and other requirements. Please note that there are restaurants on the hotel property that require dress slacks for men.
• Welcome Reception - Resort festive, slacks, collared shirt for men, casual dress or skirt for ladies.
• Celebration Dinner - Resort dress, slacks, collard shirt, for men, festive dress for ladies.

Weather: The average temperature during June is comfortable, with temperatures hovering in the upper-80s.

Check out what Cabo San Lucas has to offer!

Luxury Sailing Snorkel Race

Experience luxury and relaxation onboard a magnificent 50-foot sailing yacht. Designed for smooth sailing with modern facilities and wide berths, this unique ‘all inclusive’ adventure allows you to explore the stunning bays of Los Cabos. As we hoist the sail (relax, our professional team will do all the work), lay back and enjoy the ocean breeze as we search for a secluded bay where we drop anchor for fun activities. Soak up the sun or dive into the depths where you can enjoy amazing snorkeling with professional guides.

Dune Buggy Adventure

Hold on to your hat as we set off on an ultimate adventure through the Baja north of Cabo San Lucas in an adventure in this dune buggy excursion! Drive your authentic dune buggy through sand dunes, beach and dirt roads enjoying an adrenaline-charged tour with great views of rustic Cabo along the way!

Golf at Club Campestre

Club Campestre is a fabulous 18 hole, Nicklaus Design golf course. Spread across the gently rolling foothills of the Sierra del la Laguna Mountains, between the mountains and the sea, the course offers spectacular views from every hole. With its seamless, dramatic interplay with the raw natural beauty of the Baja California desert, this gem of a golf course will draw you back for game after game. Club Campestre San Jose invites you to enjoy the incredible panoramic views, dramatic elevation changes and undulating championship greens created specifically for us, and for this very special piece of property, by one of the great Masters of the game.

Spa

The Yhi Spa is a seductive oasis of luxury for living energetic experiences in beautiful natural surroundings. A shrine dedicated to beauty and wellbeing, where taking care of the body and soul is our priority and provided with a high quality service. The elegance of the stunning setting combines with sweet details to awaken the senses.
